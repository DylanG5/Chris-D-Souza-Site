Past Events

DEI Summit 2025 — May 22–23, 2025

The Fourth Annual D.E.I. Summit: Supporting Educators and Administrators Engaged in Equity Work took place May 22–23, 2025 at the DoubleTree by Hilton, Mississauga. In partnership with the Ontario Principals Council, the event provided solution-based programming for organizations to Recruit, Retain and Promote from marginalized employee groups.

DEI Summit — May 25–26, 2023
Developing Diversity, Empowering Equity, Inspiring Inclusion Summit

This event featured Chris as a keynote speaker where he helped to mentor Racialized & Indigenous employees, educate HR professionals and executives, helping them to recruit, retain, and promote marginalized staff. He helped to inspire change and help people to ally together in order to create inclusive, culture-rich work environments with diverse talent.

Diversity Day, Greater Sudbury, Ontario
Original articles can be found here and here

Diversity Day was an event with an important message: that we should celebrate differences rather than fear them. Chris was featured as the special guest where he spoke and witnessed his book 'Why Are All The Taxi Drivers...?' as a play after it was turned into a production by YES Theatre in Sudbury.
