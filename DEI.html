Third annual D.E.I. Summit: Mentoring Racialized and Indigenous employees for Leadership

This event will provide solution based programming for organizations to Recruit, Retain and Promote from marginalized employee groups. For additional information contact Chris D'Souza.

Timing & Location

The event will take place on October 3-4 2024, and will be located at DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Toronto Airport West 5444 Dixie Road, Mississauga, ON, L4W 2L2

Itinerary

Thursday

From 4:30pm-5:30pm we will have our keynote speaker: Deepa Ahluwalia Human Rights and Equity Advisor for the Waterloo Region District School Board; "Making Policy Work: Increasing Representation and Belonging in the Workplace ". From 5:30pm-10:00pm a dinner and social, there will be an open bar and buffet dinner followed by live music from "Some Kind".

Friday

Friday will feature our keynote speaker: Chris D'Souza, Equity and Human Rights Strategist. "Implementing a T.H.I.R.D. Rail (Transformative Hiring of Racialized and Indigenous Demographics)". This will be followed by workshop session one, lunch, and workshop session two. The summit will close with an Endnote from 2:30pm-3:30pm.
